What is DMCA Ignored Hosting?

DMCA ignored hosting refers to web hosting services located in countries where the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) is not legally enforced. Unlike U.S.-based hosting companies that must comply immediately with DMCA takedown requests, these providers operate under local laws and regulations.

This does not mean illegal activity is allowed. Instead, it means that hosting providers carefully review complaints instead of automatically suspending accounts. Many website owners suffer from false or automated copyright claims sent by competitors or bots. DMCA ignored hosting prevents unnecessary shutdowns caused by such actions.
